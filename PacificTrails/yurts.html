The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?

Our luxury yurts are permanent structures 4 feet off the ground. Each yurt is fully enclosed with canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened when wanted.

How are the yurts furnished?

Each yurt is furnished with a queen size bed with a down quilt and a gas fired stove. Your luxury camping experience includes electricity, and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.

What should I bring?

Most guests pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather conditions with light layers of clothing. It's also helpful to bring a flashlight, and don't forget a sense of adventure!
